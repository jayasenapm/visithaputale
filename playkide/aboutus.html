About Us

We are an exciting family run business located a few minutes from Toowoomba, Queensland. We are here to service Toowoomba and local surrounding areas with exceptional customer service and offer amazing all day hire rates. As parents of four children, we know the importance of making your child’s birthday party, hassle and stress free for the parents and enjoyable for all their children’s friends. Most of all the kiddies absolutely just love jumping castles and they truly make any birthday party or occasion.

We also offer a range of Personalized Party Invitation’s and Party extra’s such as VIP Personalized Lanyards with cards, Water Bottle Labels etc to select from. If you have a theme and can’t see it there no problems we also provide custom designs.

Bounce me happy will make your next celebration one to remember. Our uniquely made jumping castles sets our business apart from the rest. Our American made double slide castles which feature double slides, large jumping area and a basketball hoop are just Amazing. We also provide a great variety of popular character banners to choose from to suit your next party theme.
