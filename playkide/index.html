About Company

Bounce Me Happy are the party package specialists for Jumping Castles and party hire in Toowoomba and local surrounding areas. We offer a wide variety of exciting and fun party hire products including – Jumping Castles, Giant Soccer Dart Wall, Double Lane Waterslide, Inflatable Giant Cinema, Face Painting and Balloon Twisting Packages with Cleo the Clown, Popcorn, Slushie, Fairy Floss, Sno Cone Machines and so much more.

We are a local – Toowoomba family owned and run business offering exceptional customer service, clean and safe hire products with very competative prices whilst offering awesome party package deals. We hope you enjoy our website and if you have any questions regarding our hire products send us an enquiry form via our website today we are more than happy to answer any of your questions.
